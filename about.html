  • Scope of Project

  • With the increasing number of pet lovers around the world, there is a growing demand for a centralized platform that offers trustworthy information on pet care, nutrition, and other essential aspects of pet ownership.

  • PetConnect aims to fill this void by providing expert-approved tips, balanced diet recommendations, and easy access to nearby pet shops, all in one convenient location. Our dedication to fostering a community of pet enthusiasts will ensure that valuable knowledge is shared among fellow pet owners

  • PetConnect provides a wealth of resources on pet health, enabling pet owners to spot early signs of illness and make informed decisions about their pets' care. Additionally, users can find guidance on when to seek professional veterinary assistance.
